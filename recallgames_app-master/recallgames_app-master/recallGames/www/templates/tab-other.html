About
Project “Recall: Games of the Past – Sports for Today” is an initiative led by TAFISA and a transnational network of 13 project partners. It aims to reintroduce Traditional Sports and Games (TSG) into the daily lives of young people as a means to tackle social challenges of today – including the epidemics of physical inactivity, by documenting and disseminating TSG heritage worldwide.
Co-Funded
Project Recall was co-funded by the European Commission from the 1st of January 2015 to the 30th of June 2015.
